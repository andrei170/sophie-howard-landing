FAQ

Common Questions

How much capital do I need beyond the program?

Plan for $5K-$15K in initial inventory and launch costs. Amazon is not a zero-capital business. If you're starting with less than $5K outside of the program fee, I'd recommend saving a bit longer before enrolling.

How long until my brand makes real money?

Most students hit their first meaningful revenue month between month 4 and month 7 after enrolling. Your first $10K month is typically between month 6 and month 12. This is a real business, not a side hustle.

What's the time commitment?

10-15 hours a week in the first 6 months (research, sourcing, listing, review management). Once your first brand is running, it drops to 5-10 hours a week and you can start a second SKU.

I've read mixed reviews online. How should I think about that?

Blue Sky is a premium program and isn't right for everyone. The students who fail are usually undercapitalized or expecting drop-shipping timelines. The students who succeed are serious operators with the capital, time, and patience to build a real brand. The application call is where we figure out which group you're in, honestly.

Who is this NOT for?

Anyone looking for passive income from week one, anyone who wants drop-shipping or print-on-demand, and anyone unwilling to wait 4-6 months for real revenue. If any of those describe you, this isn't the right program.
